Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about GARO Ares—modular battery energy storage for home and professional installation.

What is GARO Ares?
Ares is GARO’s modular battery energy storage system (BESS). It combines a hybrid inverter and LFP (lithium iron phosphate) battery in one system, with 1–6 battery packs for roughly 5–30 kWh usable capacity. It’s suitable for indoor or outdoor installation (IP65) and is aimed at homeowners and professional installers in the UK and Ireland.
Do I need solar panels to use Ares?
No. You can use Ares with or without solar. Without solar, you can charge from the grid (e.g. on off-peak tariffs) and use the stored energy when rates are higher. With solar, you can also store surplus generation and increase self-consumption.
Can Ares provide backup power during a power cut?
Yes. Ares has a backup output that can keep essential loads running during a grid outage. Switching to backup typically happens in under 10 ms. The amount of backup time depends on how many battery packs you have and what loads are connected.
What warranty does Ares have?
Ares is backed by a 10-year warranty: 5 years are included as standard, with the option to extend by a further 5 years. Terms and conditions apply; details are available from GARO or your installer.
Where can Ares be installed?
Ares is designed for indoor or outdoor installation with IP65 protection. It can be wall-mounted and is suitable for garages, utility rooms, or outside against a wall. Installation must be carried out by a qualified installer in line with local regulations.
How do I get Ares installed?
Ares is installed by qualified installers. Contact GARO or visit garoelectric.com to find an installer or request more information. They can advise on system size, site requirements, and pricing for your situation.
