What is a BESS?

A BESS (Battery Energy Storage System) brings together a few key parts. Each one has a clear job so your system works safely and reliably. GARO’s BESS is all-in-one, which makes the product straightforward to install and use.

Battery

The Ares battery uses LFP (lithium iron phosphate) technology and stores electricity so you can use it later—when the sun has set or when electricity prices are high. Usable capacity from about 5 kWh up to around 29 kWh depending on system size, with 95% depth of discharge.

Inverter

The inverter converts stored DC from the battery into AC for your home. Ares models offer 3 to 6 kVA continuous power with backup output and switching to backup in under 10 ms when the grid fails.

Battery Management System

The BMS monitors and protects the battery—temperature, charge level, and health—so it works safely and lasts longer. Communication via CAN; it works automatically in the background.

Energy Management System

The EMS decides when to charge the battery, when to use stored energy, and when to use the grid. It helps you get the most from your solar and storage without extra effort.

Thermal Management System

Natural cooling keeps the battery within the right temperature range for charging and discharging (0–55°C), so it performs well and stays safe in all weather.
